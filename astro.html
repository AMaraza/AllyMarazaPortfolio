Astromando

Unreal Engine
Atlassian
Perforce

Astromando was a 4 month collaborative project. The project was based off of a reference game we collectively agreed upon, Risk of Rain 2, that we used to build our base mechanics around, and then branched out into new mechanics and features.

The game was made in Unreal Engine 4 entirely using blueprints. My primary focus was creating the enemy AI, but the whole team generally pitched in to each aspect of the game from the programming, to the level designs. Near the end of the project, we collaborated with 3D artists to add some custom made assets to our game.

For our project management tools, we used the Atlassian tool Confluence to do our documentation, and Jira for our project management using a Scrum workflow. We held frequent team meetings, as well as daily standups to keep in touch and make sure everything was running smoothly
